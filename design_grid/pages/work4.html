Prototype Highlights

  • Web-based tuner lets contributors remix audio layers in real time.
  • Visual waveform pairs with motion cues to reinforce feedback timing.
  • Inclusive presets tuned for low-hearing scenarios with vibration support.

Discovery

We mapped user reactions to existing sound cues and identified gaps where audio reinforcement could reduce task completion ambiguity.

Experiments

Paired spatial sound exploration with color and motion states to test impact on success metrics. Built a browser-based tuner so anyone could remix variations in real time.

Outcome

New sound patterns increased shortcut adoption by 25% and surfaced an inclusive baseline for acoustic accessibility guidelines.
