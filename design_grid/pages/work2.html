Motion Language Library

A documented system for transitions and product micro-interactions.

Research

Conducted motion audits across iOS, Android, and web clients to catalog existing interactions and expose inconsistencies that caused cognitive load for power users.

Framework

Authored principles anchored in physics realism and emotional tone, then paired them with implementation tokens engineers could consume alongside typography and color specs.

Deliverables

  • Interactive documentation site with After Effects embeds
  • Code sandbox for product engineers to prototype quickly
  • QA checklist to ensure new animations align with system rules
