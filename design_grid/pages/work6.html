Education Portal

A self-serve onboarding path for new design hires.

Insights

New teammates struggled to locate artifacts scattered across Notion, GDrive, and Slack. We synthesized the essentials into one guided experience.

Experience

Created modular chapters with video primers, interactive quizzes, and embedded component usage examples. Personalized pacing with progress saves per cohort.

Results

Average onboarding ramp shortened by 1.5 sprints, with higher confidence scores in our design system application assessment.
