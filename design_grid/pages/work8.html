Prototype Highlights

  • Health overview cards with real-time API status badges.
  • Anomaly timeline overlays to flag at-risk experiments instantly.
  • Live filters for cohort, platform, and risk category with saved views.

Need

Operations relied on spreadsheets to reconcile live experiment data. We needed a single, visual dashboard that surfaced risk signals in real time.

Design

Focused on clarity-first data visualization, progressive disclosure, and quick filters to isolate anomalies without overwhelming the primary view.

Success

Enabled faster pivots during experiment review, decreasing time-to-action from 48 hours to under 8 while keeping leadership aligned through shared narratives.
