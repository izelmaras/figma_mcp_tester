Studio Deck Overview

A modular presentation framework for design crits.

Problem Space

Teams needed a flexible, presentation-ready template that could scale from early concept sharing to executive readouts while preserving narrative clarity across devices.

Solution

We built a responsive layout system with interchangeable content modules, enabling designers to assemble decks quickly and adapt them for in-person or remote reviews.

  • Drag-and-drop slide modules with theme presets
  • Shared asset library synced from Figma tokens
  • Live accessibility check integrated in preview

Impact

Rollout reduced prep time for weekly critiques by 40% and created a common visual language across product squads.
