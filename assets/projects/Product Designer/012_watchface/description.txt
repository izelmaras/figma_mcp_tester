Verizon Gizmo Smart Watch for Kids

Challenge
• Improve limited functionality of current interface
• Create more advanced, engaging experience for young users
• Assist hardware design team with second generation of Verizon's Gizmo Watch

Audience
• Children aged 2 - 8
• Primary users of Gizmo Watch

Context
• Team needed vision-based work to pitch for more advanced interface
• Existing interface was limited in functionality
• Goal was to design something simple yet effective
• Challenge was to stay within Verizon's brand system while making design playful and appealing to kids
• Needed to deviate from Verizon's typical black-and-white aesthetic

Solution
• Contributed to interface designs for Gizmo Watch
• Created playful and child-friendly interfaces
• Developed art direction and concept interfaces for Gizmo Watch 2
• Focused on limited yet essential functionalities such as step tracking, tasks, and call mode
• Designed interfaces with kids in mind, ensuring they were intuitive, user-friendly, and whimsical
• Utilized Verizon's secondary color set to bring color to interfaces while staying within brand system

Results
• New interface designs envisioned delightful and engaging experience for children
• Designs integrated seamlessly with Gizmo Watch features
• Playful and colorful design maintained childlike quality, making device more appealing to young users
• Successfully part of team that brought these improvements to life

Year
• 2018

Collaborators
• Verizon Product & Gizmo Watch Team
