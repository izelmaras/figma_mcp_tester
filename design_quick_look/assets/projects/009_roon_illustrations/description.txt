Roon Illustration & Animation System

Challenge
• Create illustration and animation system embodying empathic design philosophy
• Communicate friendliness, understanding, and gentleness
• Handle sensitive content appropriately
• Provide comforting experience for users navigating challenging health conditions

Audience
• Roon's primary users: patients and caregivers
• Users dealing with complex and sensitive health conditions
• Audience needing accessible and non-triggering visual elements

Context
• Guided by principles: "playful," "empathetic," "soft," and "non-triggering"
• Visuals intended to create serene and reassuring environment
• Help users feel supported and understood
• Essential in providing comforting experience

Solution
• Developed illustration and animation system including:
  - Medically non-triggering animations
  - Soothing and straightforward visuals
  - Playful and empathetic illustrations
• Carefully crafted to ensure visually engaging and emotionally resonant experience
• Provided comfort and support throughout healthcare journeys

Results
• Successfully contributed empathy and support to Roon's platform
• Enhanced overall user experience
• Users provided endless positive feedback on illustrations
• Users expressed gratitude for welcoming environment Roon created
• Visually engaging and emotionally resonant designs created serene and reassuring atmosphere
• Significantly aided in efforts to raise seed round

Year
• 2022
