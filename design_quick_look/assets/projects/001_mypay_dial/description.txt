A circular visualization (MyPay Dial) that transforms how Chime members understand and interact with their earnings by showing time as money, making financial progress feel visible and intuitive.

Challenge
• Rethink MyPay hub: a screen that helps members understand and access their earnings with more clarity
• Explore how pay could feel more temporal and intuitive through motion and form

Audience
• Chime members seeking clarity on their earnings and financial progress

Context
• Project's mission was to rethink the MyPay hub experience
• Needed to make financial progress feel visible and understandable

Solution
• Created the MyPay Dial: a circular visualization that lets members see time as money
• Shows how earnings build day by day
• Built and thoroughly tested early interaction models in v0 prototypes
• Explored how time, value, and control could coexist in a single gesture
• Final design is now part of a pending patent application

Results
• Work reached GA (General Availability)
• Designed to make financial progress feel visible
• Successfully transformed how members understand and interact with their earnings

Year
• 2024
