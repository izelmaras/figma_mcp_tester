Roon Doctor Creator Tool

Challenge
• Scale doctor creation process on a two-sided platform
• Develop user-friendly tool for doctors with limited tech knowledge and busy schedules
• Roon's Q&A process was too time-consuming, limiting content scalability
• Riverside's interface was cumbersome and inefficient

Audience
• Doctors and medical experts creating content for Roon
• Experts who struggle to find time for answering questions
• Medical professionals seeking to share knowledge efficiently

Context
• Roon is a two-sided platform where doctors create content and patients/caregivers consume it
• Experts need efficient way to share knowledge without viewing others' responses
• Need for well-informed patient questions and reliable referral resource
• Experts want to reach broader audience without heavy social media use

Solution
• Designed tool with three modes of content creation: text, audio, and video
• Conceptualized way to view all three content forms in the same view
• Flow allows experts to schedule sessions
• Notification system alerts experts when it's time for session
• Curated questions assigned specifically to expert's background shown in a stack
• Experts can skip or record video/audio answers
• Ability to schedule next session after completion

Results
• Early user research validated key assumptions
• Doctors expressed preference for easy-to-use tool
• Tool offers flexibility with text, audio, and video formats
• Feature still in development process

Goals
• Double the number of questions recorded
• Conduct 80% of existing interviews on the app
• Increase number of experts onboarded by 40%
• Increase unique profile shares from doctors by 20%

Year
• 2024
