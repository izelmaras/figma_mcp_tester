Desen

Challenge
• Create sustainable fashion brand blending traditional Turkish craftsmanship with modern technology
• Honor rich cultural heritage of Turkish patterns while innovating design process through AI
• Explore intersection of tradition and technology

Audience
• Fashion enthusiasts who appreciate sustainable and unique designs
• People interested in mix of traditional craftsmanship and new technology
• Turkish design community

Context
• Desen is rooted in deep connection to Turkish culture
• 'Desen' means 'pattern' in Turkish, signifying intricate and beautiful patterns
• Aim to incorporate rich colors and geometric shapes of Turkish visual history
• Blend Turkish craftsmanship, Bauhaus design language, and Turkish visual history

Solution
• Co-founded Desen as family business
• Utilized AI to generate infinite patterns and designs
• Each creation uniquely Desen
• Designs inspired by timeless allure of Turkish craftsmanship, Bauhaus design language, and Turkish visual history
• Created unique, crocheted fashion pieces paying homage to past while embracing future possibilities

Results
• Gained significant attention in Turkish design community
• Featured in Turkish GQ magazine in 2023 special photoshoot
• Highlighted innovative approach and unique designs
• Currently preparing collection to be sold at Sabah Dealer's Soho and Amagansett stores
• Recognition and expansion reflect successful blend of tradition and innovation
• Resonated with both design community and fashion enthusiasts

Year
• 2022 - Current
