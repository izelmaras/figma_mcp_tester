Feature that organizes health information into "moments" to guide users through linear and non-linear health condition journeys, providing answers, checklists, and preparation guidance.

Challenge
• Organize comprehensive library of questions and present them in most accessible way
• Users overwhelmed by sheer volume of questions in system
• Not all condition journeys are linear (e.g., pregnancy is linear, cancer treatment is non-linear)

Audience
• Primary focus on users and caregivers, particularly those aged 60 and above
• Users with limited tech knowledge
• Patients and caregivers navigating health condition journeys

Context
• Through user interviews, discovered users were overwhelmed by question volume
• Users valued topical organization but desired guidance on what comes next
• Need to prepare with right information
• Some journeys are linear (pregnancy), others non-linear (cancer with multiple rounds of treatment)

Solution
• Explored both linear and non-linear layouts to map journey of condition
• Named each step in journey "moments"
• Developed delivery method providing:
  - Answers to questions
  - Checklists for what to do
  - Information on how to prepare for next steps
• Incorporated illustrative style with wiggly lines to enhance interface

Results
• Concept of moment feature successfully passed user tests
• Roon medical content and design team working to create content structures supporting feature design
• Provides guidance and structure for users navigating health journeys

Year
• 2024 - Ongoing
