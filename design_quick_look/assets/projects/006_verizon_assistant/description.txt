Verizon Assistant

Challenge
• Improve customer service and reduce volume of calls to call centers
• Develop smart assistant capable of handling key user flows through chatbot
• Address poor customer service reputation impacting Net Promoter Score (NPS)

Audience
• Verizon customers seeking customer service
• Customers who prefer to avoid store visits or phone calls
• Journey teams working on feature projects

Context
• Verizon's customer service had poor reputation
• Many customers preferred to avoid store visits or phone calls
• Significantly impacted Net Promoter Score (NPS)
• Focus on transitioning key user flows (e.g., updating plans to overseas options) to chatbot

Solution
• Developed chatbot known as Verizon Assistant
• Collaborated with journey teams to integrate essential user flows
• Oversaw handoff and componentization of chatbot
• Translated chatbot into design system
• Assisted with visual concepts and creative direction alongside agency

Results
• Well-received and widely used by Verizon customers
• Significantly reduced number of calls to call centers
• Improved customer satisfaction and operational efficiency
• Highly functional interface

Year
• 2017 - 2019
Collaborators
• Verizon Assistant Team, RG/A
