Roon Design System

Challenge
• Build comprehensive brand system from the ground up
• Create design system that supports continuous expansion of health conditions
• Need for scalable system across multiple platforms
• Ensure accessibility requirements are met

Audience
• Roon's diverse user base including patients and caregivers
• Design and engineering teams building features
• Users with varying tech knowledge, particularly those aged 60 and above

Context
• As Head of Design at Roon, needed to develop comprehensive brand system
• System needed to support launching new conditions every other month
• Required compatibility across mobile web, tablet web, desktop web, and iOS
• Needed to transform brand guidelines into practical product systems

Solution
• Developed design system using Brad Frost's Atomic Design Framework
• Created and maintained two distinct design systems over two years
• Transformed brand guidelines into practical color schemes and typography systems
• Developed bespoke set of custom icons
• Implemented latest Figma features: Auto-Layout and Variants
• Established extensive repository of symbols, templates, and pages
• Introduced light mode for enhanced user experience
• Crafted scalable navigation and illustration systems
• Ensured accessibility requirements met

Results
• Enabled design and implementation of numerous features as single designer
• Rapid iteration and enhancement of platform
• Significantly improved user experience
• Provided quick access to medically vetted information
• Catered to users' diverse needs and preferences
• System works efficiently across all platforms

Year
• 2022 - 2024
