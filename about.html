Izel Maras
Resume

Izel Maras is an interdisciplinary designer who merges craft and computation to shape how digital experiences look, feel, and move. Grounded in systems thinking, she works through prototyping, motion design, and AI-driven workflows to create expressive, functional design systems. Outside of her digital work, she draws, paints, and codes. She also founded Desen, a brand born from the dialogue between AI, Bauhaus graphic design, and handcraft.
